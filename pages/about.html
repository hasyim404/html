About Genshin Impact


On the continent of Teyvat, the people chosen by Celestia were granted a control over the power of the elements in the form of Vision. In addition, each region of Teyvat has its own Archon which is an entity that is considered a "God" and worshiped by the people of each region. The player assumes the role of a Traveler (male named Aether, female named Lumine, depending on the player's choice) who roams the world in search of his missing brother. On the way, the Traveler will meet various companions with different characters and powers, he will also be guided by a small fairy-like creature named Paimon. From the experience of meeting comrades and solving problems in the region, the Traveler will uncover the truth behind the world he is currently exploring.
