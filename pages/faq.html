F.A.Q 


  • Q1: Does the PC version of Genshin Impact support MacOS systems?
    • A1: For now, it does not.

  • Q2: Does the PC version support emulators?
    • A2: No, it does not.

  • Q3: What do I do if the game lags?
    • A3: First, check if your system settings are compatible with our system requirements (see the specifications section at the top of this FAQ). Next, you can also click on Settings in the Paimon Menu, and lower your graphics quality as needed to increase overall performance.

  • Q4: What if I am unable to download/update the game?
    • A4: First ensure that your system has enough disk space to install Genshin Impact. Next, we recommend that you use a stable internet connection with sufficient bandwidth to download Genshin Impact, as its game files are quite large.

  • Q5: Will different platforms/regions have different prices for bundles and top-up?
    • A5: Yes, there will be. Different platforms and regions have their own regulations and exchange rates, which may result in differences in bundle and top-up prices. We hope for your understanding in this matter.

  • Q6: Does the PCversion of Genshin Impact support Xbox/PS4 controllers? What do I do if I am unable to change my settings after connecting the controller?
    • A6: At present, the PC version of Genshin Impact supports the use of PS4 and Xbox One/Elite controllers. We will continue to optimize the controller experience and compatibility. Please be patient with us, Travelers! If connecting the controller renders you unable to change settings, please go to Settings > Control Panel > Devices and Printers, and check if there is a clash in your virtual controller inputs.

  • Q7: The client crashes several times during the game.
    • A7: It could be affected by other softwares in the background. After confirming that your device configuration meets the minimum requirements, please try to restart the computer before opening the client. If you still experience crashes, please contact customer service.
